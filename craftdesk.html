Frequently asked questions

Is Craftdesk free to use for business?

Yes. Every tool on Craftdesk runs directly in your browser at no cost, with no account or subscription required.

Do I need to install any software?

No. Craftdesk works entirely in a modern web browser such as Chrome or Edge, with nothing to download or install.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. Photos, videos and audio files are processed locally on your device and are never sent to an external server.

Which file formats does Craftdesk support?

Craftdesk works with common image formats such as JPG, PNG and WEBP, most browser supported video formats, and audio files that convert to WAV.
